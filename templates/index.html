Quick Start Guide

Everything you need
to get started.

A clear guide to each tool — what to enter, what you'll get back, and tips for the best results.

🧬
Career Path Predictor
Start here
01
Type your skills one at a time (e.g. python, react, sql) and press Enter or click + Add.
02
Select the domains you're interested in — AI/ML, Web, Data, Cloud etc. These personalise results.
03
Set your experience level and work style, then click Predict My Career Paths.
04
Review your ranked matches — salary ranges, growth outlook, and the exact gaps to fill.
Add at least 5–8 skills for the most accurate results. Be specific — fastapi is better than just python frameworks.
🎯
Interview IQ Scorer
01
Open the job posting and copy the full description — requirements, responsibilities, all of it.
02
Paste it into the Job Description box. Then paste your resume or skill list in the second box.
03
Click Score My Interview Readiness. You'll get an overall score and topic-by-topic breakdown.
04
Focus on topics scored below 50% — the tool shows exactly what to revise with free resources.
Use this the night before an interview. The more detailed the JD, the more accurate your score.
📄
ATS Resume Checker
01
Select the target role you're applying for from the dropdown.
02
Copy your resume as plain text and paste it in — include all sections.
03
Click Analyse My Resume. You'll get a score and an 8-dimension breakdown.
04
Scroll down to see missing keywords, section gaps, red flags, and a prioritised fix list.
Paste as plain text — formatting doesn't matter. The checker scores content, not design.
🗺️
Learning Roadmap
01
Choose the role you want to get into from the dropdown.
02
Add your current skills and set your weekly hours and timeline.
03
Click Build My Roadmap. You'll get a week-by-week plan for the skills you're missing.
04
Each week expands to show specific tasks and links to free courses and videos.
Be honest about hours — a realistic 10h/week plan beats an overpacked 40h/week plan you won't follow.

Common questions

Does CareerDNA store my resume or data?
No. Everything is processed in real-time and nothing is saved. Your resume and skills stay on your device.
Why is my Career Path score low even with many skills?
Match scores reflect alignment with a career's core requirements — not just volume. A focused set of 6 relevant skills will score higher than 20 unrelated ones.
What ATS score should I aim for?
70% or above passes most automated systems. 85%+ puts you in a strong position. Focus on Critical and High-priority fixes first.
The tool isn't responding — what should I do?
Check the status indicator in the top navigation bar. If it shows Offline, the backend service may be starting up — wait a moment and try again.
